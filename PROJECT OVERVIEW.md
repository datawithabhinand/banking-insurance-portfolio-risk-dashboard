# **PROJECT OVERVIEW**



This project analyzes customer financial profiles across banking and insurance portfolios.



The objective is to identify:

• Customer segmentation patterns

• Credit risk exposure

• Churn probability

• Insurance product distribution



DATASET



The dataset contains 500 simulated customer records, including:



\- Customer demographics

\- Credit score

\- Monthly balance

\- Loan information

\- Insurance policy details

\- Risk score

\- Customer value classification

\- Churn probability



TOOLS USED



• PostgreSQL – Data storage and SQL analysis

• Power BI – Data visualization and dashboard creation

• Generatedata.com – Dataset generation



KEY INSIGHTS



• Medium-value customers show the highest churn percentage.

• A significant portion of customers fall into the medium risk band.

• Auto and Home insurance dominate the insurance portfolio.

• Customer segmentation reveals strong mid-tier financial balances.



DASHBOARD PURPOSE



The dashboard helps financial institutions:



• Identify high-risk customer segments

• Monitor churn probability

• Understand insurance portfolio distribution

• Support strategic decision making
